How To Play

The aim of the game is to turn off all the light bulbs by using the coloured lightbulbs presented.

  1. Pressing turns on lights 4-6
  2. The light-bulbs with ID from 4-6 will all turn on, each with a different colour
  3. Once the player thinks they have turned off all the correct lights, they must click the button
  4. If the player was successful at the game, all of the lights will turn green.
  5. In order to refresh the game or start again, the player must click
  6. Then press Go for the light sequence

The player must choose the correct colour to turn off the light bulb.

  • These light bulbs act as buttons
  • The blue and red have the ID:4
  • The pink and yellow have the ID:5
  • The green and purple have the ID:6
