How To Play

The aim of the game is to turn on the right coloured light bulbs using the coloured lightbulbs presented, matching the randomly coloured boxes presented to the right

  1. First press the Go button in order to produce random colours in the boxes to the right (these will only be shown once and cannot be seen after the first light-bulb has been turned off)
  2. The light-bulbs will all turn on, each with a different colour
  3. You must remember the sequence of colours that was produced in the light grid
  4. Then click the corresponding lightbulb to turn off that lightbulb
