How To Play

The aim of the game is to turn off ALL the light bulbs by using the coloured light-bulbs presented.

  1. Once you press GO..
  2. The lights will turn on
  3. Click the correct light colour to turn off each of the light bulbs
  4. Turn off all the lights to win the game















*WARNING:*

    The light sequence may have the potential to trigger a seizure for any player who may suffer from photosensitive epilepsy
